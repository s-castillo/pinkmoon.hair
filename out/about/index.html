About

About Pink Moon Salon

Pink Moon is a salon-wellness studio space built around thoughtful hair care and genuine connection. Our single chair studio offers a comfortable place for clients to relax and unwind, without the worry of any outside interactions. This one-on-one experience helps our clientele have a more personalized service experience.

Our Story

We began with a simple belief: salon visits should feel grounding, affirming, and personal. Every appointment is approached with care, collaboration, and tailored to your goals and lifestyle.

After ten years of hair focused services, we decided to expand our offerings. Whether you choose a sensory touch service to help you unwind and be present, or an in-depth tarot or oracle consultation reading for advice or messaging — Pink Moon is a space where clients can leave feeling revitalized in more ways than one.

Our mission is to offer our clientele a place to feel connected with themselves inside and out. After doing chair rental for six years, my husband and I decided to build an at-home studio equipped with everything our clients need. Throughout the years I offered tarot readings alongside hair services, which became the origin of what is now Pink Moon.

Hair color, treatments, and cuts are transformational — and through tarot I am able to convey advice or messaging that helps personalize the overall experience. My twenty years of astrological research and knowledge greatly assists me in connecting with clients. Now with sensory services, I hope to connect with body and mind in a way that allows our clients to feel safe and refreshed.

How It Works

The Pink Moon Experience

  1. 01

    Consultation Form

    Start with your story

    Fill out our consultation form to share your hair history, goals, and what brought you to Pink Moon. This helps us arrive at your appointment prepared.

  2. 02

    A Conversation

    We connect

    Before anything else, we talk. We'll follow up to discuss your goals, answer questions, and make sure the timing and service are a great fit.

  3. 03

    Reservation

    Lock in your appointment

    Once we're aligned, we reserve your spot. Because we work by appointment only, your time is protected and fully dedicated to you.

  4. 04

    Hair Evaluation

    Assess and align

    At the start of your visit we do a hands-on evaluation — texture, condition, history, and current state — to confirm the plan and set expectations.

  5. 05

    Your Service

    The transformation

    This is where the work happens. Whether it's color, a cut, a spiritual service, or all of the above — you're in the chair with our full attention.

  6. 06

    Follow-up Reservation

    Book before you leave

    We close every appointment by scheduling your next one. Staying on a consistent cadence keeps your hair (and your energy) at its best.
